Dirt - Alice in Chains

Created: 2021-06-24 | Modified:

This album cover is okay.

I've heard this album a lot. As somewhat of a fan of the grunge era, I enjoyed the classic bands like Nirvana, The Smashing Pumpkins, Stone Temple Pilots, Silverchair (arguably post-grunge), etc... But I never got into Alice in Chains. Not out of hatred, but simply out of lack of exposure. Of course, I had heard their popular song "The Rooster", and enjoyed it. Not overly, but I thought it was intriguing.

Would is the song that made me want to listen to them. I forget how I heard it, but it was really good. Vocally, it was nice. Had a nice intro. Good bassline. The switch up towards the end is very nice as well. All around a good track.

I put on the album, and listened through it, deciding that much of is good, and much of it is (terribly) bad. But the good, shines through... Good songs: Them Bones (it's the worst of the high end), Rooster, Rain When I Die, Down in a Hole, and Would?. All else is shit. That's just the truth. I get why I've never heard of any acclaim for their albums as a whole. Much of it is a total flop. The bad is bluesy sounding, descending chord progression trash. The bad was really inconsistent from song. Variety isn't a bad thing, but in this case it is.

Besides the album, the songs that shine, shine bright. Bad album, with some good songs, is how I'd describe it in one sentence.
