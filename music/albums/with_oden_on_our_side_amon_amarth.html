With Oden on Our Side - Amon Amarth

Created: 2021-07-10 | Modified: 2021-07-18

2006. With Oden on Our Side. Amon Amarth. Their best album.

This album is the same idea as the others, but better in every musical sense: More themeatic (viking shit), melodically better, inventive rhythm sections, dynamic drumming (not boring metal drums), lyrically better (more viking shit), etc. The vocals are the same as Deceiver of the Gods, but I've always thought it was unique.

Less cringey. Yes, the album is pertaining to an obscure sub-culture (viking shit), but the way they go about it is heavier, and more inventive. Each song is about dying in combat, or has elements of it, which I find funny, but enjoy more on this album than the last.

Tracks that stand out:

  • Valhall Awaits Me
    • Okay song. It has an atypical arpeggiating guitar solo and post-chorus pieces.
  • Runes to My Memory
    • Good guitar rhythm section. Nice post-chorus going into the second guitar rhythm section.
  • Cry of the Black Birds
    • I belive this was the most successful song off of this album. It's really good. Atmospheric intro, nice lead riff throughout.
  • Prediction of Warfare
    • Nice intro: super melodic lead, and really nice drumming with stero mixing on top. The main riff loses me, but it's nice when it comes back to the intro riff later.

Favorite song: Cry of the Black Birds
