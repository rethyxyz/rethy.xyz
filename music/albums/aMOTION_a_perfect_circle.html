aMOTION - A Perfect Circle

Created: 2021-07-02 | Modified: 2021-07-18

aMOTION. 2004. An underestimated and unheard of compilation. Truly, it's a collection of remixes of the previous album (from the year 2000), Mer de noms.

A collection of tracks by a whole plethora of (at least to me) unheard artists (I'm probably just ignorant to them). The only reason I occasionally come back to this album is for 3 Libras (Feel My Ice dub mix). The song is a good way to test audio devices because of its heavy use of depth, stereo, and other effects. Plus, it's just a good remix.

Favorite song: 3 Libras (Feel My Ice dub mix).
