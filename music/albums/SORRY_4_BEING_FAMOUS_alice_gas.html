SORRY 4 BEING FAMOUS - Alice Gas

Created: 2021-06-24 | Modified: 2021-07-18


Terrible album cover as per most LPs I review on this website. Seriously, what the fuck is this garbage?

A pretty alright album. Defined as Hyperpop (it's really multigenere, spanning across a multitude of electronic ones).

I wish it was more consistent. It seems half of each song is music, while the remainder is unlistenable distortion, or whatever other abrasive noise they chose. If it wasn't a factor, I'd consider the album to be well mixed. Through, I will give the artist the benefit of the doubt here. I try to do that, as I probably just missed what they were going for with that particular feature.

People refer to FERRARI as the defining song, at least from what I've heard, but I don't think so. For me, it's too humorous, and I rarely choose to listen to it, though it isn't bad. RUN IT UP is what I believe is the best. It features least of the "funny" attitude of the other songs, and doesn't have the unlistenable distortion stuff. It's a good song standalone.

Fuck the cringe fanbase, though. That goes for all Hyperpop.

Favorite song: RUN IT UP.
