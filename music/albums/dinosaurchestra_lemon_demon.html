Dinosaurchestra - Lemon Demon

Created: 2020-12-16 | Modified: 2021-07-18

A album guaranteed to be unknown to anyone living outside of the internet during the mid-2000s (even if you were, it's still unlikely).

A nostalgic album from old school internet content creator Neil Cicierega, who has went on to create an whole assortment of music/short films ranging from Harry Potter Puppet Pals to The Ultimate Showdown of Ultimate Destiny (which is found on this album; track #6).

He's least notably-but-still-importantly know for old school computer animations created from collections of GIFs and JPEGs called Animutations. His creations landed him on the news at the age of (I believe) 14, his first taste of Internet virality.

Structurally, the album is simple. Typical time signatures and rhythms. Most of the outcome is predictable, as you can expect where the next section will go and how it will sort of sound. This isn't a bad thing, as there isn't too over simplification. Now, where its inventiveness come in, is through its subject matter and actual instrumentation.

Lets take the lyrics for an example: The song Stuck (track 5) is about Neil (the creator of the album) being stuck in a song (this song, to be specific). It's actually quite humorous. Have a listen.

That's just one example, but most songs have this sort of superficial-introspective-ness.

Favorite song: This Hyper World or Deep in the Ocean
